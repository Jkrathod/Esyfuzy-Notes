What is OAuth Misconfiguration?

The most in famous OAuth-based vulnerability is when the configuration of the OAuth service 
itself enables attackers to steal authorization codes or access tokens associated 
with other users’ accounts. 
By stealing a valid code or token, the attacker may be able to access the victim’s account.
